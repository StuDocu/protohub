Neutral prompts — keep it going without leading

  • › Walk me through your thinking.
  • › What did you expect there?
  • › What would you do next?
  • › Say more about that.
  • › Silence — wait 5 seconds, let them fill it.

Do not

  • ✕ "Do you like it?" / "Isn't this useful?"
  • ✕ Use our feature names before they do (readiness check, spine, temporal chip).
  • ✕ "It's supposed to…" — don't defend. Log the confusion.
  • ✕ Rescue them at the first pause. Silence is where reactions come from.
